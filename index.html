JS Cards

Robo JS Dealer

This started off as a homework assignment that required doing calculations of blackjacks scores. I was unable to find an open source way to shuffle and display cards, so I made this.

This page will let you draw 3 cards and evaluate those cards as a blackjack hand, or allow you to draw 5 cards from a shuffled deck.

The shuffled deck, once exhausted, will re-shuffle and continue.

The cards come from here: https://opengameart.org/content/playing-cards-vector-png

Repo where the code & cards reside: https://github.com/sword20/sword-js-cards
